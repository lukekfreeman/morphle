How to Play Morphle

Transform the starting word into the destination word by swapping one letter at a time. You can rearrange the letters after each swap. Each intermediate word must be a valid 5-letter word.

Rules:

  • Only one letter can change from the previous word.
  • You can rearrange the letters after the change.
  • You have 10 guesses to reach the destination word.
  • Use the on-screen keyboard or your physical keyboard to enter words.

Color Indicators:

  • G - Correct letter in the correct position.
  • Y - Correct letter in the wrong position.
  • G - Letter not in the destination word.
